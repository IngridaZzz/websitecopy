Professional guidance and wellness awaits you

Welcome to our bright, welcoming lounge close to the cozy cobbled of Sorø Torv.

Bruuns Frisør offers the best for your hair when it comes to professional guidance as well as cutting, dyeing, permanent and extensions.

Wellness and relaxation are important so you get a good experience. Therefore, you are offered massage in the delicious white leather massage chair while your hair is washed and cared for.

Immerse yourself in glossy magazines and colored weeklies while the lids are set. Or, borrow one of our iPads if you're into fresh news or updates on Facebook. There is of course a wide selection of water, coffee and tea as well as chocolate while you are delicious.

You can park for free in the parking lot behind the salon when you drive to Roland.
